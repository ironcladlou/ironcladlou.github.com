Visualizing community group geographic distribution

Sat, Jul 1, 2017 maps, go

Our county 4-H club members are widely distributed geographically. Visualizing the member distribution with a heatmap may be useful to inform decisions which affect members in ways impacted by the location of members’ homes.

The member data is mildly anonymized by generating random coordinates within a certin radius of family home address locations.
